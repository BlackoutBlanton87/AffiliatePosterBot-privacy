Privacy Policy

This app, AffiliatePosterBot, does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. It operates solely to automate affiliate link posting and engagement tracking on social media platforms.

We respect user privacy and comply with Facebook's platform policies. No user data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising purposes.

All interactions with Facebook are handled through secure, permission-based access tokens. Users may revoke access at any time via their Facebook settings.
